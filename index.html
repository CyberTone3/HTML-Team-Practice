A Little Something For Your Sweet Tooth 🥧

Apple-Cranberry Galettes

This sweet savory pastry is perfect for a holiday dessert or if you simply want a little something to excite your taste buds. These beautiful, open-faced pastries are absolutely delicious.

Prep-Time:40 minutes Cook Time:55 minutes Serving:12

Ingredients:

For the pastry:

  • 1 3/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 6 Tbs. white conrmeal
  • 2 tsp. sugar
  • 3/4 tsp. salt
  • 3/4 cup cold unsalted butter, cut into small pieces
  • 6 Tbs. sour cream
  • 1/2 cup ice water

For the filling:

  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 3 Tbs. honey
  • 3 Tbs. fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 tsp. ground cinnamon
  • 8 large apples such as Golden Delicious, McIntosh or other tart baking apple, about 4lbs, peeled, cored and sliced
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh cranberries
  • 2 Tbs. unsalted butter, cut into thin slices
  • Creme fraiche (optional)
  • Fresh thyme springs for garnish (optional)
  • Flaky sea salt for garnish (optional)
  • Honey for drizzling (optional)
